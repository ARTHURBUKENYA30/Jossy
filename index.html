About Us

At jossy snacks we bring you fresh, tasty, and affordable snacks made with love in bwerenga entebbe. Perfect for any occasion our snacks will satisfy your cravings. Jossy Snacks is a one stop snack bar. Dealing in variety of snacks,biscuits,sweets,gums,chocolate and many more both fresh and packed. We also make birthday cakes, wedding cakes, cupcakes, cookies, pizzas, donuts, kebabs, egg rolls, samosas, pies, sausages, sandwiches and many more. We also make packed dry snacks for students to be taken to school like: gnats, cookies, bagiya, crisps, daddies, gonja and many more. We also have snacks packed for school visitation and we also help in planning and visiting students at school for parents with no time for VDs.
